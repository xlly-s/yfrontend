Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is YTDownHub?
YTDownHub is a free online tool that allows you to download videos from YouTube in various formats and qualities. You can save videos as MP4 (video), MP3 (audio), or other available formats.
2. Is YTDownHub free to use?
Yes, YTDownHub is completely free to use with no hidden charges. We may display ads to support the service, but there are no premium features or paywalls.
3. How do I download YouTube videos?
Simply paste the YouTube video URL into the input field on our homepage and click "Download". Then select your preferred format and quality, and the download will start automatically.
4. What video qualities are available?
Available qualities depend on the original video, but typically include:
  • 144p, 240p, 360p, 480p (SD)
  • 720p (HD)
  • 1080p (Full HD)
  • 4K (when available)
5. Can I download just the audio?
Yes! Choose the MP3 format to download only the audio track. We offer various bitrates for optimal quality.
6. Is this service legal?
YTDownHub operates as an independent tool. The legality of downloading YouTube videos depends on your local laws and how you use the content. We encourage users to respect copyright laws and only download videos they have rights to or for personal use under fair use provisions.
7. Why does my download fail sometimes?
Download failures can occur due to:
  • Invalid or private YouTube URLs
  • Server overload during peak times
  • Network connectivity issues
  • YouTube API changes
Try refreshing the page or checking the URL if this happens.
8. Do you keep copies of downloaded videos?
No, YTDownHub does not store any downloaded content on our servers. Videos are processed in real-time and delivered directly to your device.
